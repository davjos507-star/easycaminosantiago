What is the Camino de Santiago?

A network of ancient pilgrimage routes converging at the Cathedral of Santiago de Compostela, in Galicia, Spain. Declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it is walked by over 400,000 people worldwide each year in search of spirituality, adventure and a digital detox.
