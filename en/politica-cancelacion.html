Cancellation Policy

At Easy Camino Santiago we understand that plans can change. This cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent, protecting both our clients and our ability to provide the best possible service.

1. Cancellations by the Client

If you need to cancel your booking, the applicable charge depends on how much time remains before the service begins:

Cancellation timeframe Charge Refund
More than 60 days before Loss of deposit (30%) 70% of total amount
Between 60 and 30 days 50% of total price 50% of total amount
Between 29 and 15 days 75% of total price 25% of total amount
Fewer than 15 days 100% of total price No refund
No-show 100% of total price No refund

Important: Timeframes are calculated from the start date of the first booked service (first accommodation, first transfer, etc.).

2. Cancellation Procedure

To process a cancellation correctly:

  1. Send your cancellation request in writing to info@easycaminosantiago.com or via WhatsApp at +34 982 907 629.
  2. Include your full name, booking reference number and reason for cancellation.
  3. You will receive confirmation of the cancellation within 24 business hours.
  4. Any applicable refunds will be processed within a maximum of 14 business days via the original payment method.

3. Date Changes

If you need to change the dates of your booking (subject to availability):

  • More than 45 days in advance: Free of charge up to 2 times per booking. From the third change onwards, a handling fee of €30 applies.
  • Between 45 and 20 days: A handling fee of €50 per change, plus any price differences for the accommodation.
  • Less than 20 days: A date change is treated as a cancellation and new booking, with the corresponding cancellation charges applied.

4. Cancellation Insurance

We strongly recommend taking out travel insurance that covers cancellation for justified reasons (illness, accident, death of a family member, etc.). At Easy Camino Santiago we can help you find the most suitable option for your situation.

The following reasons for cancellation are typically covered by travel insurance:

  • Illness or accident affecting the insured person or a close family member
  • Death of a family member up to the second degree
  • Involuntary redundancy
  • Jury or court summons
  • Serious damage to the primary residence

5. Cancellations Due to Force Majeure

In the event of extraordinary and unforeseeable circumstances beyond the control of either party (WHO-declared pandemics, natural disasters, armed conflicts directly affecting the route), Easy Camino Santiago will offer, as a priority:

  • Date change at no additional charge
  • Credit voucher for future bookings for the full amount paid
  • In exceptional cases, a partial refund following individual assessment

6. Cancellations by Easy Camino Santiago

In the extraordinary event that Easy Camino Santiago must cancel a confirmed service, the client will be entitled to:

  • Full refund of the amount paid within 14 business days, or
  • An equivalent alternative of similar or superior quality at no additional cost.

Easy Camino Santiago accepts no responsibility for additional costs the client may have incurred independently (flights, additional accommodation not managed by us, etc.).

7. Contact for Cancellations and Changes

For all matters relating to cancellations or changes, please contact us:

  • Email: info@easycaminosantiago.com
  • WhatsApp: +34 982 907 629
  • Office hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 – 19:00 (CET)
